The life and career of Bad Bunny

Bad Bunny who was born in 1994 is a Puerto Rican rapper, singer, and songwriter. He is known for his deep voice, top music, and weird fashion he has become one of the most influential Latin artists of all time and shaping reggaeton and Latin trap globally.

Early Life and Career

He grew up in Vega Baja, Puerto Rico and was influenced by reggaeton icons like Daddy Yankee. While he was studying communications at the University of Puerto Rico he uploaded songs to SoundCloud. For example his track “Diles” got attention in 2016 which led to a signing with a top music label and the beginning of his career.

Bad Bunny Hits

Since his start in the industry, Bad Bunny has become a global famous superstar. His first hit album which was Un Verano Sin Ti (2022) that topped the Billboard rankings. Also, he has collabed with artists like Drake, J Balvin, and Cardi B, and has sold out tours around the world.
