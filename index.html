The Life and Career of Bad Bunny

Bad Bunny, born in 1994, is a Puerto Rican rapper, singer, and songwriter. He is known for his deep voice, hit music, and unique fashion style. He has become one of the most influential Latin artists of all time, shaping reggaeton and Latin trap globally.

Early Life and Career

He grew up in Vega Baja, Puerto Rico, and was influenced by reggaeton icons like Daddy Yankee. While studying communications at the University of Puerto Rico, he uploaded songs to SoundCloud. His track “Diles” gained attention in 2016, leading to a record deal and the start of his professional career.

Bad Bunny Hits

Since his start in the industry, Bad Bunny has become a global superstar. His first hit album was Un Verano Sin Ti (2022), which topped the Billboard charts. He’s collaborated with artists like Drake, J Balvin, and Cardi B, and has sold out world tours.
